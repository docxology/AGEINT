# Transparency Notice Workflow: public-facing purpose, safeguards, and limits

The AGEINT transparency notice converts internal evidence into a plain-language
record that a learner, instructor, public reviewer, or affected community can
inspect.

## Workflow: draft, review, publish boundary, and retain notice evidence

| Step | Required evidence |
|---|---|
| Public purpose | lawful educational, governance, or defensive purpose; authority; affected service; and decision role |
| Tool and data summary | model, data, supplier, provenance, validation, accessibility, and sensitivity summary |
| Impact and review | benefits, risks, safeguards, human review, appeal or escalation route, and contact point |
| Publication decision | publish, partially publish, delay, or hold decision with exemption rationale |

The workflow is grounded in directly verified public-sector transparency
sources including the UK Algorithmic Transparency Recording Standard, OMB
M-25-21, the Federal AI use-case inventory model represented by NARA's AI
inventory, Canada's federal AI Register, OECD public-sector AI governance, and
OECD open-government guidance. Sensitive details can be de-sensitized, but
accountability fields must remain reviewable.
