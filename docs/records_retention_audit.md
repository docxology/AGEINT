# Records Retention and Audit: retained evidence, owners, and deletion rules

AGEINT records retention is evidence-minimal and review-complete: retain what a
future reviewer needs to reproduce, challenge, and remediate an artifact, while
avoiding unnecessary sensitive material.

## Retained records: prompts, sources, decisions, incidents, and remediation

| Record | Required fields |
|---|---|
| Source and prompt register | source identity, prompt version, tool allowlist, reviewer, timestamp, and caveat |
| Decision and exception log | risk owner, accepted exception, compensating control, expiry date, and approval |
| Artifact retention note | output type, sensitivity, access boundary, deletion rule, and refresh trigger |
| Incident and remediation record | incident signal, containment action, root cause, owner, retest result, and closure date |

NARA AI use-case inventory material, the NARA 2025 AI compliance plan, OMB
M-25-21, OECD AI incident-reporting guidance, CISA OT asset-inventory guidance,
and CISA definitive OT architecture guidance anchor the public-sector records
and auditability angle. Generated curriculum artifacts should remain synthetic,
public, owned-lab, or instructor-provided unless a separate approved
data-governance review exists.
