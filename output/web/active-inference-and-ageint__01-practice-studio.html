Free-energy bound. Variational free energy \(F\) upper-bounds surprise \(-\ln p(o)\) for observations \(o\) and hidden states \(s\) under a recognition density \(q(s)\): \[F = D_{\mathrm{KL}}\!\big(q(s)\,\|\,p(s \mid o)\big) - \ln p(o) \;\ge\; -\ln p(o).\] Because the Kullback-Leibler term is non-negative, minimizing \(F\) over \(q(s)\) tightens the bound on surprise – the precise statement of this lesson’s plain-language warning about a model explaining too much from too little evidence [scholarly_friston_2010_fep]; [scholarly_buckley_2017_fep_mathematical_review].
